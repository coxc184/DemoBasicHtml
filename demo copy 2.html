The peach is very popular; only the apple and pear have higher production amounts for temperate fruits

The peach (Prunus persica) is a deciduous tree that bears edible juicy fruits with various characteristics.
Most are simply called peaches, while the glossy-skinned, non-fuzzy varieties are called nectarines. Though from the same species, they are regarded commercially as different fruits.
